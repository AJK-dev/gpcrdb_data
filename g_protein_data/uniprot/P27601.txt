CC   -!- FUNCTION: Guanine nucleotide-binding proteins (G proteins) are
CC       involved as modulators or transducers in various transmembrane
CC       signaling systems.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: G proteins are composed of 3 units; alpha, beta and
CC       gamma. The alpha chain contains the guanine nucleotide binding
CC       site. Interacts with UBXD5. Interacts with HAX1. Interacts (when
CC       active) with PPP5C (via TPR repeats); activates PPP5C phosphatase
CC       activity and translocates PPP5C to the cell membrane (By
CC       similarity). Interacts with RGS22 (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- INTERACTION:
CC       O08915:Aip; NbExp=3; IntAct=EBI-2255627, EBI-6935014;
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Membrane {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18703424};
CC       Lipid-anchor {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18703424}. Melanosome
CC       {ECO:0000250}. Cytoplasm {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18703424}. Nucleus
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18703424}. Note=Cytoplasmic in adult somatic
CC       cells, but mainly nuclear in spermatids in the testes.
CC       Translocates from the cytoplasm to the nucleus during
CC       spermatogenesis, hence predominantly observed in the cytoplasm of
CC       round spermatids but localized in the nuclei of elongating or
CC       elongated spermatids and testicular spermatozoa.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in brain and testis, as well as in
CC       kidney and sperm (at protein level).
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18703424}.
CC   -!- PTM: Phosphorylation on Thr-203 destabilizes the heterotrimer of
CC       alpha, beta and gamma, and inhibits Rho activation. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the G-alpha family. G(12) subfamily.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
