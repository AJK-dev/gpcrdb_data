CC   -!- FUNCTION: Guanine nucleotide-binding proteins (G proteins) are
CC       involved as modulators or transducers in various transmembrane
CC       signaling systems.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: G proteins are composed of 3 units; alpha, beta and
CC       gamma. The alpha chain contains the guanine nucleotide binding
CC       site. Interacts with UBXD5. Interacts with HAX1. Interacts (when
CC       active) with PPP5C (via TPR repeats); activates PPP5C phosphatase
CC       activity and translocates PPP5C to the cell membrane. Interacts
CC       with RGS22. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15339924,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16202387, ECO:0000269|PubMed:18703424}.
CC   -!- INTERACTION:

CC       P32302:CXCR5; NbExp=9; IntAct=EBI-465387, EBI-2835269;
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Membrane; Lipid-anchor. Melanosome.
CC       Cytoplasm. Nucleus. Note=Identified by mass spectrometry in
CC       melanosome fractions from stage I to stage IV. Detected in the
CC       cytoplasm of Leydig cells and in the seminiferous epithelium,
CC       including differentiating cells from the spermatogonia to mature
CC       spermatozoa stages. In round spermatids, also present in the
CC       nuclei.
